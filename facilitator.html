Facilitator guide — how to use these results
OPEN THE DEBRIEF
Show the live matrix. Let the team see where the majority land — this opens honest conversation without singling anyone out.
CELEBRATE ALL FOUR
Every quadrant has value. Champions drive momentum, Learners bring energy, Sceptics add rigour, Observers provide balance.
FOCUS YOUR TIME
Spend the most debrief time on the two largest quadrants. Address the top concern from Sceptics and Observers explicitly.
CLOSE WITH A COMMITMENT
Ask each group: "What is one thing you will do in 30 days?" Write it on a shared board. Ownership starts with a public declaration.
MIX THE GROUPS
Use the Grouping tab to form mixed-quadrant teams. Diversity of AI attitude within a group drives richer peer learning.
FOLLOW UP
Share the anonymised matrix with all participants after the session. Transparency builds trust and sustains the conversation.
