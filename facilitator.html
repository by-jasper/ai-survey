The Six Thinking Hats method (Edward de Bono) structures group discussion so everyone thinks from the same perspective at the same time. The 👑 Royal Hat is our addition — the group facilitator. Share this page link with participants so they can read their own hat before the session: hats.html

👑
Royal Hat
Facilitator · 1 per group
  • Guides the group through each hat
  • Manages time per hat
  • Ensures no voice dominates
  • Records commitments at close
SAY:
  • "Let's hear Yellow hat now"
  • "Time to move to Green"
  • "Quick Red hat check before we close"
🤍
White Hat
Facts & Data
  • Focus on data only
  • No opinions or feelings
  • State what is known
  • Identify information gaps
ASK:
  • "What does the data say?"
  • "What facts do we have?"
  • "What information is missing?"
❤️
Red Hat
Emotions & Feelings
  • Share gut reactions freely
  • No justification needed
  • Intuition is valid here
  • Feelings are information
ASK:
  • "How does this make you feel?"
  • "What's your instinct?"
  • "What excites or worries you?"
💛
Yellow Hat
Benefits & Optimism
  • Find the positives
  • Explore best-case outcomes
  • Play the optimist role
  • Look for opportunity
ASK:
  • "What's the best outcome?"
  • "What value does this unlock?"
  • "What has already improved?"
💚
Green Hat
Ideas & Creativity
  • Generate new ideas freely
  • No idea is too wild
  • Challenge assumptions
  • Quantity over quality
ASK:
  • "What if we tried this?"
  • "What if there were no limits?"
  • "What completely new approach works?"
💙
Blue Hat
Process & Planning
  • Focus on structure
  • Define next steps
  • Organise the thinking
  • Opens and closes discussions
ASK:
  • "What is our first step?"
  • "How do we prioritise?"
  • "What is the 30-day plan?"
🖤
Black Hat
Caution & Judgement
  • Identify risks and problems
  • Logical caution — not pessimism
  • Protect against mistakes
  • Ask the hard questions
ASK:
  • "What could go wrong?"
  • "What biases might appear?"
  • "What are we missing?"
Suggested AI readiness debrief sequence (approx 30 min)
👑
Royal — Open (2 min)
  • Share survey results
  • Explain hat sequence
  • Set discussion goal
💙
Blue — Agenda (2 min)
  • Agree on today's focus
  • Set time per hat
🤍
White — Facts (5 min)
  • Review scatter matrix
  • Note quadrant counts
  • State avg score
❤️
Red — Feelings (3 min)
  • One word per person
  • No justification
  • Record the mood
💛
Yellow — Upsides (4 min)
  • What excites us about AI?
  • What's already working?
🖤
Black — Risks (4 min)
  • Name the real concerns
  • Bias, privacy, job fears
💚
Green — Ideas (5 min)
  • What should we try first?
  • Solutions to Black hat risks
💙
Blue — Commit (5 min)
  • One action per person
  • Set next check-in date
